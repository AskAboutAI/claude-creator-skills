# Claude Creator Skills

A collection of 8 ready-to-use Claude skills built specifically for **content creators, personal brands, and online coaches** who want Claude to work like a dedicated creative team — not a generic AI assistant.

Curated and shared by **Raj Gyanani — [AskAboutAI](https://www.youtube.com/@AskAboutAI)**

---

## What Are Claude Skills?

Claude skills are plug-in instruction files (`.skill`) that transform Claude into a specialized assistant for a specific task. Instead of explaining what you want Claude to do every single time, you upload a skill once and Claude automatically knows *exactly* how to behave, what framework to follow, and what output to produce.

Think of each skill as hiring a specialist. These 8 skills turn Claude into your content research analyst, voice coach, scriptwriter, repurposing engine, analytics debriefer, bio writer, and visual director — all in one place.

---

## How to Install a Skill in Claude

1. **Download this repo** — click `Code → Download ZIP` on GitHub, then unzip the folder
2. **Open Claude Code** (desktop app or claude.ai/code)
3. **Go to Skills** in the settings/sidebar
4. **Click "Add Skill"** and upload the `.skill` file you want
5. That's it — the skill is now active in your Claude session

You can upload one skill at a time or all 8. Each skill activates automatically when Claude detects you need it.

## The 8 Skills

### 1. `audience-research-analyst.skill`
**Find exactly what your audience is saying — before you create anything.**

Digs through comments, Reddit threads, DMs, YouTube comments, and niche forums to surface the exact phrases your audience uses, their real fears (not just surface complaints), and the questions they're silently asking. Outputs a full Audience Intelligence Report with 10–15 steal-ready phrases and 8–12 content ideas rooted in real data.

**Trigger phrases:** "what does my audience actually want?", "analyze these comments", "find pain points", "research my audience", "give me content ideas from real people"

---

### 2. `brand-voice-guardian.skill`
**Make sure everything you publish sounds like YOU — not like AI.**

Analyzes your existing content across 6 dimensions (sentence rhythm, vocabulary fingerprint, emotional temperature, I vs. you balance, structural habits, what you never say) and builds a Voice Card. Then applies it as a filter to every new piece — flagging anything that sounds too polished, too corporate, or too generic. Because in your world, voice IS the brand.

**Trigger phrases:** "does this sound like me?", "make it sound more like me", "my content sounds too AI", "write in my style", "this doesn't feel right"

---

### 3. `content-ideation.skill`
**Never stare at a blank screen again.**

Mines content ideas from 7 sources: audience pain points, your personal story, contrarian angles, behind-the-scenes moments, frameworks you use, niche gaps nobody's filling, and trending reactions. Delivers a prioritized idea bank sorted into "Make This First", "Queue These", and "Parking Lot" — so you always know what to post next.

**Trigger phrases:** "I don't know what to post", "give me content ideas", "I'm out of ideas", "what should I make this week?", "brainstorm ideas", "content plan"

---

### 4. `content-performance-debriefer.skill`
**Turn your metrics into a clear creative direction.**

Takes your post analytics — reach, saves, shares, comments, watch time, profile visits — and produces a clean weekly debrief: the winner, the lesson, what patterns are working, what to kill, and one specific next move. Builds a running scorecard so you improve every single week instead of guessing.

**Trigger phrases:** "analyze my content performance", "what worked this week?", "my reach dropped", "this post flopped, why?", "weekly content review", "what should I post more of?", shares analytics screenshots

---

### 5. `content-repurposer.skill`
**One piece of content. Ten to fifteen outputs. Nothing lost in translation.**

Takes a YouTube video, podcast, blog post, or long caption and explodes it into platform-native pieces — Reels, carousels, captions, email, Twitter/X threads, LinkedIn posts — each one built for its platform, not copy-pasted from the original. Includes a posting order suggestion so one video fuels 2–3 weeks of content.

**Trigger phrases:** "repurpose this", "turn this into more content", "what else can I make from this", "break this down into posts", "I have this video/blog/podcast", "make this into reels"

---

### 6. `killer-scripter.skill`
**Scripts that feel like a friend talking — not a content creator performing.**

The most comprehensive skill in the pack. Writes YouTube long-form scripts, Shorts/Reels scripts, and Instagram/LinkedIn carousel copy. Includes 50 plug-and-play hook templates, 9 storytelling structures (Hero's Journey, Man in a Hole, The Breakthrough, etc.), the BUT/THEREFORE framework for body writing, and a full tone checklist. Every script sounds human, specific, and impossible to scroll past.

**Trigger phrases:** "write a script", "write a reel", "make a carousel", "YouTube script", "short-form script", "write slides for this", "how should I say this on camera"

---

### 7. `offer-and-bio-writer.skill`
**Every word on your profile is either working for you or costing you.**

Writes Instagram bios (3 versions: result-first, story-first, and provocative), link-in-bio page copy, full 7-section landing pages, and DM scripts for cold outreach, warm follow-ups, and inquiry responses. Built to convert attention into action — not just look good.

**Trigger phrases:** "write my bio", "fix my Instagram bio", "my bio isn't converting", "write a landing page", "help me write my link in bio", "write a DM script", "how should I pitch my offer"

---

### 8. `visual-storyteller.skill`
**Turn any script into a complete visual plan — even without a film crew.**

Reads a script or brand story and outputs: a visual style direction (Personal Brand or Cinematic Realism), a full Art Direction Brief, a shot-by-shot table with shot types and camera movements, image prompts for Midjourney/Leonardo, and video prompts for Kling/Runway. Everything a creator needs to film with intention instead of winging it.

**Trigger phrases:** "make this visual", "plan the shots for this", "what should this look like on camera", "shot breakdown", "art direction", "help me visualize this script", "create image prompts"

```
        ↓

        ↓

        ↓
content-performance-debriefer → Analyze what worked the following week
        ↓

```
